About Me

Hello! I'm Nitz, a 24-year-old from Israel. I created this Chocobo-themed vacation tours site as part of my studies at John Bryce.

The inspiration for this site comes from the Final Fantasy series, a renowned franchise in the world of video games. Within this series, Chocobos are iconic, bird-like creatures often used as a mode of transportation across various terrains. These friendly and versatile creatures have become a beloved symbol of the series, representing adventure and exploration.

About the Website

My website combines the whimsical theme of Chocobos with real-world vacation tours. The site features an intuitive design, allowing users to easily browse through different tour options and discover exciting destinations.

The frontend uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a visually appealing and responsive experience. The backend is powered by Python with Flask, managing user accounts, tour details, and bookings securely through a MySQL database.
